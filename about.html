About Giselle

Product Designer

My work isn’t (usually) flashy by accident. I design to solve real problems, align teams, and move products forward. I care less about trends and more about clarity, usability, and decisions that hold up under pressure.

I work best in complex environments, when requirements are messy, constraints are real, and the path forward isn’t obvious. That’s where I can add the most value.

What I’m good at

  • Breaking down ambiguous problems into clear, actionable design decisions.
  • Thinking in systems, not screens: flows, logic, edge cases, and tradeoffs.
  • Designing with intent, where every choice has a reason behind it.
  • Collaborating with product and engineering to ship realistic, scalable solutions.
  • Advocating for users without losing sight of business and technical constraints.

I’m comfortable owning problems end-to-end, from understanding the “why” to shaping the “how.”

Background

Bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design and Media Arts, Southern New Hampshire University.

What you get working with me

  • A designer who asks the right questions early.
  • Someone who reduces risk, not adds noise.
  • A partner who helps teams move forward with confidence.

If you’re looking for a designer who can think critically, work independently, and bring clarity to complex products, the best way to reach me is on LinkedIn .
